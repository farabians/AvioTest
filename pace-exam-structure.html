PACE Exam Structure

The PACE Exam is made up of 9 modules. The exam is completed in three sections, with 10 minute breaks in between.

Modules Overview

Sustained Attention

Audio-Visual Memory

Digit Span

Agility

Three-Dimensional Spatial Perception

Spatial Orientation

Physics

Mathematics

MIC (Joystick)

Section 1

This section includes 3 modules:

  • Sustained Attention
  • Audio-Visual Memory
  • Digit Span

After completing these modules, there is a 10-minute break.

Section 2

This section includes 3 modules:

  • Agility
  • Three-Dimensional Spatial Perception
  • Spatial Orientation

After this section, there is another 10-minute break.

Section 3

This is the final section and includes 3 modules:

  • Physics
  • Mathematics
  • MIC (Joystick Module)

This section completes the PACE exam.
