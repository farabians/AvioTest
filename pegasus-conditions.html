Pegasus Pilot Program – Application Requirements

Please review the following criteria carefully before applying.

  • Turkish Citizens,
  • Bachelor's degree from formal education graduate programs of Turkish Universities or Certificate of Equivalence for graduates of Foreign Universities (YÖK Denklik Belgesi),
  • Born on and after 1.01.1991,
  • Have no criminal record (Adli Sicil ve Adli Sicil Arşiv Kaydı),
  • Have no military affiliation for at least 2 years.

English Proficiency Exam will be executed by Pegasus. However, the following English proficiency test results obtained within the last 2 years with the indicated minimum or above scores will be accepted:

IELTS General or Academic

Overall: 6 - Speaking: 6 - Listening: 6 - Reading: 6 - Writing: 6

TOEFL IBT

Overall: 75 - Listening: 22 - Speaking: 22 - Reading: 20 - Writing: 20

ITEP Academic Plus (under supervision in test center)

Overall: 3,5 - Listening: 3,5 - Speaking: 3,5 - Reading: 3 - Writing: 3 - Grammar: 3

  • Be able to meet the medical prerequisites for Airline Pilots (Class-1 Medical Certificate),
  • Height between 160cm-195cm and fit to Pegasus BMI standards.
  • Have not participated in any pilot training program before (PPL, CPL, etc.).
