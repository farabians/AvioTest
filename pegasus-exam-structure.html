The 3 Phases Pattern

This pattern applies to all modules in the Pegasus exam.

Phase 1 — 3 minutes (Training Mode — Not Graded)

  • This is a training phase.
  • Difficulty is easy.
  • You GET feedback (correct/incorrect), and you see your reaction time.
  • You can SKIP this phase at any time using the button on the top-right.
  • Phase 1 is NOT graded.

Phase 2 — 3 minutes (Graded Training Phase)

  • It is similar to Phase 1, with a similar difficulty.
  • You GET feedback and see your reaction time.
  • You CANNOT skip this phase.
  • Phase 2 is GRADED.

Phase 3 — 10 minutes (Main Test — Graded)

  • This is the main part of the exam.
  • It is graded.
  • You do NOT get feedback.
  • You do NOT see reaction time during the test.
  • The difficulty starts easily and becomes harder over time.
  • Phase 3 is GRADED and longer than the other phases.

Note: There is NO break between modules.
