What is Spatial Orientation 2D?

This test checks how well you read short instructions and find the correct path on a 2D grid. You will be presented with a series of directional commands that you must follow mentally to reach a final destination.

1. Instructions

2. Propositions

How to Play

  • You will see written instructions on the screen.
  • Follow the path mentally on a virtual 2D grid.
  • You will then see 4 propositions (options).
  • Each option has a green start point and a red end point.
  • Important: The green point always starts facing north (up).
  • Your task is to follow the instructions, find the correct endpoint, and choose the matching option.

Module Phases
