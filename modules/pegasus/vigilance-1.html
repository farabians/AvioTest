What is Vigilance 1?

This test measures your sustained attention through two simultaneous tasks: a visual tracking task and an auditory monitoring task. It checks how well you stay focused during repetitive activities.

Visual & Auditory Demo

Short demo video: Dial movement and sound tones

Interaction Buttons

Eye Button (Visual) | Music Button (Auditory)

1. Tracking Task (Visual)

  • You will see a dial with two parts: an analog dial (like a clock) and a digital number.
  • Both move together most of the time.
  • Sometimes, the digital number changes, but the analog dial does NOT change.
  • When this happens, press the Eye button.
  • ⚠️ This happens rarely. The test checks how well you stay focused.

2. Auditory Task

  • You will hear three different tones during the test.
  • If the same tone plays 3 times in a row, you must press the Music button.
  • Stay alert for the sequence even while tracking the dial.
