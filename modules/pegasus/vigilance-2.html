What is Vigilance 2?

In this test, a small ball moves around a circle. Most of the time, the ball simply turns in the same rhythm. However, it is designed to be mesmerizing, testing your ability to stay alert for rare "special events."

GIF: Ball moving in a circle

Special Events

Color Change | Shape Change | Jump | Direction Change

Special Events

Watch for these four specific actions:

  • Color Change: The ball changes its color briefly.
  • Shape Change: The ball changes its shape (e.g., to a square).
  • Jump: The ball skips a step in its circular path.
  • Direction Change: The ball reverses its rotation direction.

Your task is to watch the ball carefully and press the correct button for the event you see.

The Challenge

The ball can turn normally for a long time without doing anything. This is on purpose — it is meant to mesmerize the test taker and make them miss the event if their attention drops.
