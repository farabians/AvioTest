Digit Span

This test measures your short-term memory and attention.

How it works

You will see a sequence of numbers shown one by one on the screen. Numbers range from 1 to 9. Your task is to remember only certain numbers based on a simple rule:

  • Numbers appear one after another.
  • You compare each new number with the previous number.
  • If the new number is higher, you keep it in mind.
  • If the new number is lower, you ignore it.

Example:

7 → 6 → ignore 6

6 → 7 → keep 7

7 → 6 → ignore 6

6 → 8 → keep 8

At the end, you are asked to enter the numbers you kept, in the correct order.

1. Number Sequence

Numbers appearing one by one (1-9)

2. Input Phase

Enter the "kept" numbers in order

Questions

  • The test includes 8 questions.
  • The sequence length increases gradually as the test goes on.
  • Each question becomes more challenging than the previous one.

Phases

Training Phase

  • Short practice questions
  • Not graded
  • Helps you learn the rule

Exam Phase

  • 8 graded questions
  • Increasing difficulty
  • No help during the questions
