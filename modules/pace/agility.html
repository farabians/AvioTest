Agility

In this test, shapes move on the screen at different speeds toward a reaction area.

What is Agility?

In this test, shapes move on the screen at different speeds. You will see circles and squares in red or green. They move upward or downward toward a reaction area in the middle of the screen.

1. Moving Shapes

GIF: Red/Green circles and squares moving toward center

2. Reaction Area

GIF: Shape touching the reaction line and button press

Your Task

  • Press the correct button as soon as a shape touches the reaction area line.
  • You do not need to wait for the full shape to enter the area.
  • Press the button when it first touches the line.
  • Always follow the latest instruction shown on the screen.

Button Rules

  • Left → Red Circle
  • Right → Green Circle
  • Up → Red Square
  • Down → Green Square
  • Note: These rules may change during the test.

Important Notes

  • Do not press too early or too late.
  • Some shapes do not enter the reaction area. Do not press any button for these shapes.
  • Only your first response is recorded.
  • Once saved, a response cannot be changed.
