Mathematics

This test evaluates your mental arithmetic skills and your ability to solve mathematical problems quickly and accurately.

What is the Mathematics Module?

The Mathematics module tests your ability to perform mental calculations and solve word problems. You will need to be proficient in basic operations, percentages, ratios, and simple algebra.

Mental Math Example

"15% of 240 = ?" or "Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 13"

Key Topics

  • Arithmetic: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
  • Percentages & Ratios: Calculating discounts, growth, and proportions.
  • Algebra: Solving simple linear equations.
  • Geometry: Basic area, volume, and angle calculations.

Module Details

Speed

You must solve questions quickly as time is limited.

Accuracy

Avoid simple mistakes under pressure.

No Calculator

All calculations must be done mentally or on scratch paper.
