Physics

This test assesses your knowledge of basic physical principles and their application in aviation-related scenarios.

What is the Physics Module?

The Physics module consists of multiple-choice questions covering fundamental topics such as mechanics, thermodynamics, electricity, and fluid dynamics. The focus is on understanding concepts rather than complex calculations.

Sample Question

Diagram: A lever system or a circuit diagram

Key Topics

  • Mechanics: Forces, motion, energy, and work.
  • Fluid Dynamics: Pressure, buoyancy, and Bernoulli's principle.
  • Thermodynamics: Heat transfer and gas laws.
  • Electricity: Basic circuits, voltage, and current.

Module Details

Format

Multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

Time Limit

Approximately 20-30 minutes for the entire section.

Difficulty

High school level physics with an emphasis on logic.
