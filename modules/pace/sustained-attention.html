Sustained Attention

In this test, you will watch colored circles moving across the screen and react based on specific rules.

What is Sustained Attention?

In this test, you will watch colored circles moving across the screen. There is a gray area in the middle. When a circle enters this area, it becomes invisible but continues moving in the same direction. Below the gray area, there are three buttons: Red, Black, and Yellow.

2. Reaction Buttons

GIF: Red, Black, and Yellow buttons below the area

How it Works

  • Circles move toward and through the gray area.
  • Lines may appear inside the gray area.
  • Each line gives a different rule for when to press a button.
  • You must estimate the correct moment and press the matching button.

Rules You May See

  • Vertical colored line: Press the matching color button when the same-color circle intersects the line.
  • Horizontal colored line: Press the matching color button when the same-color circle exits the gray area.
  • Black line: Press the black button when two circles connect with each other.
